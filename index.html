Department of ICT

Government department responsible for information and communication technology in Bangladesh.

The Department of Information and Communication Technology was established on 1 July 2013 as part of the Bangladesh Awami League governments digital Bangladesh policy under the Ministry of Posts, Telecommunications and Information Technology. The department plans to establish ICT infrastructure across Bangladesh.
